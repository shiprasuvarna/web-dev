Roller Skating

Roller skating is travelling on surfaces with roller skates.
It is a reacreational activity and a sport.
Roller rinks and skate parks are built for roller skating,though it also takes place on streets, sidewalks, and bike paths.
There are different types of skating like Ice Skating, Inline Skating, Roller Skating, Outdoor Skating.

Benefits of Skating

Skating benefits the whole body – it works on the legs and glutes, helps flex and tone several areas that include abs, thighs, and also calves. It helps stabilize the Achilles tendon. It supports the ankle as it works harder while roller skating. Not to mention,the arms too contribute to maintaining speed and balance. Skating works nearly every muscle group in the body, and gliding requires synchronized movement of the legs, which is important for joint flexibility. It also builds up the leg and abdominal muscles. Like any workout, skating is great for cardiovascular health – it gets blood pumping and the heart rate up. So if you want to stay fit sign up for our skating classes now!!!
